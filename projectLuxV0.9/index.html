Project Lux is the new way to take control of your home. With this web app, you never have to wonder if you turned out that light. Now you can see if the lights are on and if they need replacing. save power by only using the lights when you are in the room or if the room becomes too dark. With project Lux and this web app, your home is now with you where ever you go.
